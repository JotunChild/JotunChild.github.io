1. League of the South (LoS)
Ideology: Neo-Confederate, white nationalist, and separatist.
Headquarters: Killen, Alabama.
Key Leader: Michael Hill, co-founder and president.
Activities: The LoS advocates for a free and independent Southern republic and has organized rallies and conferences,
including flash demonstrations in Wetumpka and other locations.

2. United Klans of America (UKA)
Ideology: Ku Klux Klan (KKK), white supremacist.
Historical Significance: Once the most violent Klan organization in the 1960s and 1970s, the UKA was linked to several
high-profile hate crimes, including the 16th Street Baptist Church bombing.

3. Patriot Front
Ideology: White nationalist, alt-right.
Activities: Known for distributing propaganda and organizing flash demonstrations. In June 2023, members protested a Pride event in Prattville, Alabama.

4. Occidental Dissent
Ideology: White nationalist.
Location: Eufaula, Alabama.
Activities: Operates as a platform for promoting white nationalist views.

5. Council of Conservative Citizens (CCC)
Ideology: White nationalist.
Location: Eufaula, Alabama.
Activities: Historically known for promoting segregationist and white supremacist ideologies.
