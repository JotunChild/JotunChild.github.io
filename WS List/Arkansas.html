2. Knights Party (Ku Klux Klan)
Location: Harrison, Arkansas
Leader: Thom Robb
Ideology: White supremacy, segregation, Christian nationalism

3. ShieldWall Network
Location: Mountain View, Arkansas
Leader: Billy Roper
Ideology: White nationalism, white separatism
Activities: The ShieldWall Network is a white nationalist organization led by Billy Roper. It aims to establish a white ethno-state and has been involved in organizing events and distributing materials to promote its ideology.

4. Patriot Front
Location: Statewide, Arkansas
Ideology: White nationalism, American nationalism
Activities: Patriot Front is a national white nationalist group that has been active in Arkansas, engaging in activities such as distributing propaganda and organizing flash demonstrations to promote its views.

5. New Aryan Empire (NAE)
Location: Russellville, Arkansas
Leader: Wesley Gullett
Ideology: White supremacy, prison gang culture
Activities: The New Aryan Empire is a white supremacist prison gang that has operated in Arkansas. Members have been involved in criminal activities, including drug trafficking and violent acts.
