1. 1488s
Type: Prison-based neo-Nazi gang
Symbols: The number "1488" refers to white supremacist codes; "14" stands for the "14 Words" slogan, and "88" represents "Heil Hitler" (H being the 8th letter of the alphabet). Members often wear a tattoo depicting an Iron Cross superimposed over a swastika.
Activities: Engaged in violent crimes, including racketeering, kidnapping, and murder.

2. Patriot Front
Type: White nationalist organization
Activities in Alaska: Known for distributing propaganda and organizing flash demonstrations.
